Lily loved to play in the garden. She'd spend hours exploring the colorful flowers, giggling with her friends, and building towers of bright red and yellow. One sunny afternoon, she noticed a little girl sitting by the old oak tree. 

Lily's eyes lit up. She sat down beside the tree and gently touched the rough bark. "Hello there," she whispered, her voice like the rustling of leaves.

The little girl looked up, her eyes wide with wonder.
