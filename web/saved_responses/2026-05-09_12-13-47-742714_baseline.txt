A rainy day is a period of overcast, often gloomy, and often wet conditions. It can bring a refreshing coolness and a sense of peace, but it can also be a time of hardship and uncertainty. 

Here are some key characteristics of a rainy day:

*   **Cloudy skies:** The atmosphere is usually clear and bright, with a slight drizzle or mist.
*   **Rainy and wet:** The rain is often heavy and can make the ground slippery.
