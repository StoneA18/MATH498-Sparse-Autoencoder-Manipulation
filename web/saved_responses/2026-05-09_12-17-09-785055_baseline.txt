The life of a snowman is a fascinating and often overlooked aspect of human history. It's a story of resilience, adaptation, and the enduring power of the human spirit. Here's a glimpse:

*   **Origins and Development:** Snowmen are not born overnight. They are formed over centuries, often from the remains of animals, particularly deer, or even the remains of human settlements. They are adapted to survive in harsh environments, such as cold, wet, and windy conditions.
