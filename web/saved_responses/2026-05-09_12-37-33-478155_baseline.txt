The temperature on a typical day can vary quite a bit depending on several factors, including:

*   **Time of year:** Summer is generally hotter and bluer, while winter is typically colder and bluer.
*   **Location:** The temperature varies significantly by region.
*   **Weather conditions:** Sunny days are common, while cloudy days can lead to cooler temperatures.
*   **Activity level:** People who are active or engaged in outdoor activities tend to be warmer.
