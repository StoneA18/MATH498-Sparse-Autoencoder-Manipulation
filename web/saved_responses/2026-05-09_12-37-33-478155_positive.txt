The temperature on a typical day can vary quite a bit depending on several factors, including:

*   **Time of year:** Summer is generally hotter and bluer, while winter is typically cooler and snowier.
*   **Location:** The temperature varies significantly by region.
*   **Weather conditions:** Sunny days, cloudy days, and even extreme weather events can affect temperature.
*   **Personal preference:** Some people prefer warmer temperatures, while others prefer cooler temperatures.
