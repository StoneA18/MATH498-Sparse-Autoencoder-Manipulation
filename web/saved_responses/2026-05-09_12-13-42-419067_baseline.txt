A rainy day is a period of overcast and often gloomy weather. It can bring a refreshing coolness and a sense of peace, but it can also be a time of hardship and uncertainty. 

Here are some key characteristics of a rainy day:

*   **Cloudy skies:** The atmosphere is often thick with clouds, creating a sense of dampness and a feeling of being watched.
*   **Rainy and gloomy:** The weather is often described as gloomy, with a sense of sadness
