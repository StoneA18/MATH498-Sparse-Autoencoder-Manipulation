The sun was warm on Elara's face as she sat on the weathered stone bench, the scent of lavender and woodsmoke drifting in from the nearby village. She was tending to the roses, her hands a gentle rhythm of work. A robin, perched on a nearby branch, chirped a cheerful greeting. 

Elara smiled, a genuine smile that reached her eyes. She knew the robin was a gift, a reminder of the beauty that bloomed in the quiet corners of the world
