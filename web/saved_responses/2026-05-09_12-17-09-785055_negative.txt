The life of a snowman is a fascinating and often comical one. Here's a glimpse:

*   **Appearance:** Snowmen are typically tall and slender, with a wide, often exaggerated, face. Their bodies are often covered in a thick layer of snow, giving them a frosty, almost ethereal appearance. They have long, flowing hair that often resembles a snowdrift, and their eyes are bright and expressive.
