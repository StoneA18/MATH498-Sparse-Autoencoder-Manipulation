A child walked across a strange land where glacier ice met hot sand and broken concrete. The sky shifted from bright gold to deep violet, with a rainbow arc fading into smoke. A shark shape moved under clear water beside a cactus, while a small robot lay half buried in dust.

Music echoed faintly, something like rock n roll mixed with drums and static. A helicopter passed overhead, loud and sharp, then gone. The child stepped over glass, wire, and bone, then onto soft grass filled with tiny flowers.

Nearby, a mirror stood alone, reflecting not the land but a city of neon lights and tall towers. A butterfly landed on it, then vanished. The air smelled like salt, fire, and rain all at once.

Far away, a volcano glowed red, while snow fell gently nearby. A train rushed past with no tracks, fast and silent. The child felt fear, wonder, and calm together.

The ground hummed like quiet energy. Shapes shifted, colors bent, and time felt loose. The child kept walking, through chaos and order, through heat and cold, through something both simple and infinite.
